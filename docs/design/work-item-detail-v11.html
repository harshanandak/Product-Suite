v2.3 — Payments revamp

At risk · ·
Objective → KR · KPI payment-success 99.2%

Every PSP we added over the last two years brought its own checkout embed, its own webhook shape, and its own retry semantics. v2.3 consolidates all of it behind a single checkout, tokenization and webhook stack — one integration surface instead of five — and ships the unified payments dashboard that finance has been asking for since Q2.

The revamp is sequenced so we can keep taking payments the whole time: the new stack runs shadow-first, then takes 10% of checkout traffic behind a flag at Beta, and only replaces the legacy path once the exit criteria hold for a full week.

Scope

  • Unified checkout — one hosted flow across PSPs, with 3-DS fallback for EU cards.
  • Tokenization & vault — card-on-file migration to the new vault, with rotation.
  • Webhooks v2 — signed, idempotent delivery with a shared retry queue.
  • Payments dashboard — settlement, disputes and refund state in one place.

Out of scope

Apple Pay dynamic descriptors moved to v2.4, and no pricing or fee changes ride along with this release. Fraud-rule tuning landed as research and will be productized separately.
